GulabJamun

Ingredients

  • Cardamom powder
  • Rose Water
  • Sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Khoya (reduced milk)
  • Baking soda
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, maida, and a pinch of baking soda.
  2. Gradually add milk to form a smooth dough.
  3. Make small, smooth balls from the dough.
  4. Heat ghee in a pan for deep frying.
  5. Fry the dough balls until golden brown.
  6. Make sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water.
  7. Add cardamom powder and rose water to the syrup.
  8. Soak fried balls in the sugar syrup for a few hours.
  9. Gulab Jamuns are ready to be served!

Soan Papdi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Besan
  • Gram flour (besan)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Pistachios or almonds (optional for garnish)

Recipe

  1. Mix besan and ghee to form a crumbly texture
  2. Cook the besan mixture on low heat until it changes color and becomes fragrant
  3. Make sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water
  4. Add cardamom powder to the sugar syrup and bring it to a one-string consistency
  5. Pour the sugar syrup into the besan mixture and mix well
  6. Quickly spread the mixture onto a greased surface
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes and then cut it into square or diamond shapes
  8. Allow it to cool completely before separating the pieces.
  9. Garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds if desired.
  10. Store in an airtight container once completely cooled.

Kaju katli

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Cashews

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Make a sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water over low heat.
  3. Add cashew powder to the sugar syrup and mix well.
  4. Cook the mixture on low-medium heat, stirring continuously.
  5. Cook until it forms a thick dough-like consistency.
  6. Add a spoon of ghee and continue to cook until the mixture leaves the sides of the pan.
  7. Transfer the mixture to a greased surface and roll it into a thin, even layer.
  8. Cut into diamond or square shapes while it's still warm.
  9. Allow it to cool completely before removing the pieces.
  10. Kaju Katli is ready to be served and enjoyed!
